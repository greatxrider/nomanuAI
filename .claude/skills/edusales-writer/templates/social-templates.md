# Social Media Templates

## Twitter/X Templates

### Template 1: The Thread Framework

```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
[BOLD CLAIM or CURIOSITY HOOK]

[PROMISE - "Here's the [number]-step process:" or "A thread:"]

---

Tweet 2-6 (Value):
[NUMBER]. [STEP/TIP NAME]

[Explanation in 1-2 sentences]

[Optional: example or proof]

---

Tweet 7 (Bridge + CTA):
That's the framework.

[WHAT YOU BUILT/OFFER that implements this]

[CTA: Link, comment prompt, or retweet ask]

---

Tweet 8 (Engagement boost):
Found this helpful?

• Retweet the first tweet to help others
• Follow @[handle] for more [TOPIC] content
```

---

### Template 2: The Single Tweet (Insight + Product)

```
[INSIGHT or HOT TAKE]

[BRIEF EXPLANATION or PROOF]

[NATURAL BRIDGE]: "That's why I built [PRODUCT]" or "This is what [PRODUCT] does"

[Optional: link or CTA]
```

---

### Template 3: The "Before/After" Thread

```
Tweet 1:
[YEAR] me: [OLD/BAD APPROACH]
[YEAR] me: [NEW/GOOD APPROACH]

Here's what changed (thread):

---

Tweet 2:
The problem with [OLD APPROACH]:

• [ISSUE 1]
• [ISSUE 2]
• [ISSUE 3]

It wasn't sustainable.

---

Tweet 3-5:
What I do now:

[STEP/INSIGHT with brief explanation]

---

Tweet 6:
The result?

[TRANSFORMATION/RESULTS]

---

Tweet 7:
I've [packaged/automated/built] this into [PRODUCT].

[CTA]
```

---

### Template 4: The Contrarian Tweet

```
Unpopular take: [CONTRARIAN STATEMENT]

Most people think [COMMON BELIEF].

But [EVIDENCE OR REASONING].

[WHAT YOU'RE DOING ABOUT IT - ties to product]
```

## Instagram Caption Templates

### Template 1: The Carousel Hook + Caption

**Carousel Structure:**
- Slide 1: Bold hook/title
- Slides 2-7: Value points (one per slide)
- Final slide: CTA + branding

**Caption:**
```
[HOOK that matches Slide 1]

Here's the truth: [INSIGHT]

[BRIEF CONTEXT - why this matters]

Save this for later and share with someone who needs it.

---

Want to [ACHIEVE RESULT]?

[PRODUCT MENTION with how it helps]

Link in bio to [CTA]

.
.
.
[HASHTAGS - 5-10 relevant ones]
```

## Short-Form Video Script Template

```
**HOOK (0-3 seconds):**
[PATTERN INTERRUPT - question, bold claim, or visual hook]
"Stop [doing thing] if you want [result]"
"The reason you're not [achieving thing]..."
"This changed everything for me:"

**PROBLEM (3-10 seconds):**
[AGITATE the pain point]
"Most people [wrong approach]"
"You've probably tried [failed solution]"

**SOLUTION (10-40 seconds):**
[TEACH the framework/insight]
"Here's what actually works:"
[Step-by-step or key points]

**BRIDGE (40-50 seconds):**
[CONNECT to your offer naturally]
"This is exactly what [product] does..."
"I built [product] to solve this..."

**CTA (50-60 seconds):**
[CLEAR next step]
"Link in bio"
"Comment [KEYWORD] for the guide"
"Follow for more [topic]"
```

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X:
- Shorter paragraphs (often single sentences)
- More casual/punchy tone
- Threads for education, single tweets for hot takes
- Quote tweets for engagement

### Instagram:
- Visual-first (caption supports the image/video)
- Longer captions OK (but front-load the hook)
- Heavy use of line breaks
- Hashtags matter for discovery

### LinkedIn:
- See linkedin-templates.md for full breakdown
- Professional but personable
- Story-driven performs well
- Engagement bait works (polls, questions)

### TikTok:
- Hook in first 1-3 seconds or you're dead
- Face-to-camera builds trust
- Trending sounds boost reach
- Education + entertainment = best combo
